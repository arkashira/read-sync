# Marketing Plan for Read-Sync

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning:**
"Self-hosted, ad-free e-book reading platform with seamless cross-device sync."

**Alternative Positionings:**
1. "Privacy-focused e-book reader with real-time sync across all your devices."
2. "The ultimate e-book companion: read anywhere, anytime, without ads."
3. "Sync your reading progress effortlessly with Read-Sync, your personal e-book hub."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profiles)
1. **Alex the Avid Reader**
   - **Daily Job:** Reads multiple e-books weekly, uses multiple devices.
   - **Budget:** $10-$20/month for premium reading features.

2. **Sarah the Student**
   - **Daily Job:** Reads academic texts and novels, needs to switch between devices.
   - **Budget:** $5-$15/month for a reliable reading platform.

3. **Mark the Privacy Advocate**
   - **Daily Job:** Prefers self-hosted solutions, values privacy and ad-free experiences.
   - **Budget:** $15-$30/month for a secure and private reading platform.

## Channels
1. **Reddit (r/eBooks, r/selfhosted)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $5-$10 per acquisition.
   - **Strategy:** Engage in discussions, share user testimonials, and offer exclusive deals.

2. **Twitter (X) Influencers**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $10-$15 per acquisition.
   - **Strategy:** Partner with book influencers for sponsored posts and reviews.

3. **Email Marketing**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $3-$7 per acquisition.
   - **Strategy:** Target existing e-book readers with personalized email campaigns.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-2:** Teaser posts on social media, blog introduction to self-hosted reading solutions.
- **Week 3-4:** Detailed feature breakdowns, user testimonials, and comparison with competitors.
- **Week 5-6:** Launch announcement, live demos, and influencer collaborations.
- **Week 7-8:** User guides, troubleshooting tips, and community engagement.
- **Week 9-12:** Success stories, feature updates, and customer feedback sessions.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize beta testing, gather user feedback, and prepare marketing materials.
- **D-0:** Official launch on all platforms, press release, and social media blitz.
- **D+30:** First major update based on user feedback, influencer partnerships in full swing.
- **D+90:** Expand to new markets, introduce premium features, and analyze success metrics.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Aim for 1,000 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR Target:** Achieve $5,000 MRR by D+90.
