 ```markdown
# Customer-Journey.md

## Aware

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer discovers the read-sync e-book reading platform through online search, social media, or word-of-mouth.
- Customer experiences frustration with current e-book reading solutions, such as limited cross-device syncing, intrusive ads, or lack of self-hosted options.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty understanding the unique value proposition of read-sync compared to existing solutions.
- Lack of awareness about the self-hosted, ad-free nature of the platform.

### User Emotions
- Frustration with current e-book reading solutions.
- Curiosity about read-sync's unique offering.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear and concise communication of read-sync's unique value proposition.
- Demonstration of the seamless cross-device syncing feature.

### Metric
- Increase in website traffic and engagement from potential customers.

## Consider

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer visits read-sync's website or social media profiles for more information.

### Friction Points
- Complexity of the platform's features and benefits.
- Lack of clear pricing information or trial options.

### User Emotions
- Interest in the platform's unique offering.
- Skepticism about the platform's ability to deliver on its promises.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Easy-to-understand explanations of the platform's features and benefits.
- Clear pricing information and trial options.
- Testimonials or case studies from satisfied customers.

### Metric
- Increase in website engagement (time spent on site, page views, etc.) and lead generation (contact form submissions, newsletter sign-ups, etc.).

## Try

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer downloads the read-sync app or sets up a self-hosted instance.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in installation and setup process.
- Technical issues during the trial period.

### User Emotions
- Excitement about trying a new e-book reading solution.
- Anxiety about the installation and setup process.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Streamlined installation and setup process.
- Responsive customer support during the trial period.
- User-friendly interface and intuitive navigation.

### Metric
- Increase in trial sign-ups and app downloads.
- Decrease in support tickets related to installation and setup issues.

## Adopt

### Trigger Event
- Potential customer decides to adopt read-sync as their primary e-book reading solution.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in migrating existing e-books to the read-sync platform.
- Technical issues during the adoption process.

### User Emotions
- Relief at finding a solution that addresses their pain points.
- Satisfaction with the platform's features and performance.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Seamless migration of existing e-books to the read-sync platform.
- Personalized onboarding and support during the adoption process.

### Metric
- Increase in active users and revenue.
- Decrease in support tickets related to migration and adoption issues.

## Expand

### Trigger Event
- Adopted customer discovers additional features and benefits of the read-sync platform.

### Friction Points
- Difficulty in finding and understanding additional features.
- Lack of integration with other e-book sources or devices.

### User Emotions
- Excitement about discovering new features and benefits.
- Frustration with limited integration options.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Clear communication of additional features and benefits.
- Integration with popular e-book sources and devices.
- Personalized recommendations based on the user's reading history.

### Metric
- Increase in user engagement and retention.
- Increase in revenue through upselling and cross-selling.
```
