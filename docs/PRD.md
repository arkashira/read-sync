# Product Requirements Document: Read-Sync
## Overview
Read-Sync is a self-hosted, ad-free e-book reading platform designed to provide a seamless reading experience across devices, including e-readers and phones. This document outlines the product requirements for Read-Sync, focusing on solving the problem of fragmented reading experiences and providing a unified solution for users.

## Problem Statement
E-book readers often face the challenge of managing their reading positions across multiple devices. This can lead to a frustrating experience, as users may lose their place or struggle to keep their reading progress in sync. Existing solutions often rely on cloud services, which may raise concerns about data privacy and ads.

## Target Users
The primary target users for Read-Sync are:

* Avid e-book readers who use multiple devices (e-readers, phones, tablets) to read their books
* Users who value data privacy and prefer ad-free experiences
* Individuals who seek a seamless reading experience across devices

## Goals
The primary goals for Read-Sync are:

1. **Seamless Syncing**: Provide a reliable and efficient syncing mechanism for reading positions across devices
2. **Data Privacy**: Ensure that user data remains private and secure, without relying on cloud services or displaying ads
3. **User Experience**: Offer an intuitive and user-friendly interface for managing e-book libraries and reading progress

## Key Features (Prioritized)
The following features are prioritized for the initial release of Read-Sync:

1. **Device Syncing**: Implement a syncing mechanism that allows users to access their reading positions across devices (e-readers, phones, tablets)
2. **E-book Library Management**: Provide a user-friendly interface for managing e-book libraries, including adding, removing, and organizing books
3. **Reading Position Tracking**: Develop a system for tracking reading positions, including support for multiple formats (e.g., EPUB, PDF, MOBI)
4. **User Authentication**: Implement a secure user authentication system to protect user data and ensure privacy
5. **Settings and Customization**: Offer settings and customization options for users to personalize their reading experience (e.g., font sizes, themes)

## Success Metrics
The success of Read-Sync will be measured by the following metrics:

1. **User Adoption**: The number of users who adopt Read-Sync as their primary e-book reading platform
2. **User Retention**: The percentage of users who continue to use Read-Sync over time
3. **Syncing Reliability**: The percentage of successful syncing events across devices
4. **User Satisfaction**: Feedback and ratings from users, indicating their satisfaction with the platform

## Scope and Out-of-Scope
The scope of Read-Sync includes:

* Developing a self-hosted, ad-free e-book reading platform
* Implementing device syncing and reading position tracking
* Providing a user-friendly interface for managing e-book libraries and reading progress

Out-of-scope features include:

* Integrating with cloud services or third-party platforms
* Developing a mobile app (initial release will focus on web and desktop applications)
* Supporting audio books or other multimedia formats

By focusing on the key features and goals outlined in this document, Read-Sync aims to provide a seamless and private e-book reading experience for users, setting a new standard for the industry.
